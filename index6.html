Band Details
Established in 2004, A group of teenagers formed a band under the quote from pirates of the caribbean “Bring me the horizon” thus their name.
They started off as a DeathCore Band that slowly evolved in a hybrid mix of Rock and Pop in 2019. Their first album was named "The Bedroom Season" that focused on the more gritty side of Deathcore...
